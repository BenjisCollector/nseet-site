Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 20, 2026

Nseet ("the App") is a days counter and tracker for iOS. Your privacy is fundamental to how we built this app. The short version: we don't collect any of your data.

Data Storage

All data you create in Nseet — events, countdowns, settings, and preferences — is stored exclusively on your device. Nothing is transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party.

No Accounts

Nseet does not require or offer user accounts. There is no sign-up, no login, and no profile information collected.

No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or process any personal information. Specifically:

  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No crash reporting sent to us
  • No advertising or ad identifiers
  • No location data
  • No contacts, photos, or other device data

WidgetKit

Nseet includes home screen and lock screen widgets powered by Apple's WidgetKit. The widget extension accesses your event data through an App Group, which is a secure on-device data sharing mechanism provided by iOS. No data leaves your device through this process.

In-App Purchases

Nseet offers optional in-app purchases processed entirely by Apple through StoreKit. We do not receive, store, or have access to your payment information, billing details, or Apple ID. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for information about how Apple handles purchase transactions.

Third-Party Services

Nseet does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics services, or advertising frameworks.

Children's Privacy

Since Nseet does not collect any data from anyone, it is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy, we will update this page with the new date. Given our commitment to not collecting data, we do not anticipate significant changes.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at hello@nseet.app.
